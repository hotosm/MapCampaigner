WHAT IS THIS?

The OSM Campaign Manager project aims to bring field data collectors and campaign managers together on a single platform.

Campaign managers are confederate insights from various platforms to evaluate progress on field campaigns.

Field data collectors are encouraged to participate in a campaign close to them.
